📚 How Online Classes Work

Follow these simple steps to access your class materials:

  1. 📅 Check Your Schedule: Look at your assigned class date below
  2. 📄 Download Materials: Click on document links to download worksheets and resources
  3. 🎥 Watch the Video: Follow along with the instructional video
  4. ✏️ Complete Exercises: Work through the provided exercises at your own pace
  5. ❓ Ask Questions: Contact your teacher if you need help or clarification
